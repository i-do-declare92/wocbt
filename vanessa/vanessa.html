    Vanessa - Children's Tour Guide

    I grew up tracking wild animals through the forests of the Canadian Shield. This rural environment provided the opportunity to examine First Nations petroglyphs, find arrowheads and other primal Aboriginal tools, sparking an intense interest in archaeology.


    These days, I live in a large city to spend more time helping others to learn about the Creator and what he promises for the future. I started serving as a regular pioneer in 2021.
