    • Some 3,000 years before the advent of modern science, the Bible book of Genesis opened with perhaps the most famous words ever penned: “In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.”

    Using the simplest of language, the historian Moses went on to detail the gradual formation of the Earth. This ancient record continues to be the center of hot debate to this day among people of science and faith, even within their own institutions. But can we trust the Bible as a reliable source of information? Just how accurate is the Bible’s account of creation?

    Join us as we explore chronologically the history of the Earth and of Life. As we tour the incredible fossil collection of the Chicago Field Museum’s “Evolving Planet” exhibit we will see the remarkable accuracy with which the Bible described the development of our planet.
