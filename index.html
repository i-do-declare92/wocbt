    How Much Do Tours Cost?

    We offer our tours FREE of charge because we want everyone to benefit from the information provided without imposing a financial burden on anyone during these difficult times.

    However, we do suggest a donation of $5 per person per tour, as these tours are our full-time employment. This helps to offset the cost that goes into the time, energy, and production of these tours. Accepted methods for donating are provided at the conclusion of each tour; you can also find them in the tour guide bios on the “Donate” page. Thank you for your support!

    How Does It Work?

    Click on any of the “Book a Tour” buttons and follow the prompts. Choose your time zone and a date, and select the number of connections you wish to book for from the “quantity” box. Further details and login instructions will be provided on your confirmation page.

    We update our tour schedule each month, only booking tours one month at a time. New tour dates are announced on Instagram @WondersOfCreationTours. If you wish to book a private tour for a large group of more than 100 connections, please reach out to us via the “Contact” page.
