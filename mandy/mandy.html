    Mandy - English Tour Co-Host

    With a rich, spiritual heritage harkening back to 1921, a stop over at Yankee Stadium in 1958 and an entire life time fascinated with Jehovah‘s creation, Mandy loves seeing how the Bible and Jehovah’s other Book, Natural Science, harmonize so well.

    Her love of history in all its forms has allowed her the great satisfaction of being able to share what Jehovah has made for us with all of you and anyone that will listen. She’s known for assisting during the English adult tours, but also does research and helps with scheduling.

    Privileged to be carer for her 90 year old Grandma, she also loves handcrafts like woodworking, drawing, knitting, sewing, and trying to keep her poor plants alive.
